How Our Shipping Works

We ship all orders from our base in Dublin, Ireland. Our shipping costs are calculated based on your location within Ireland to ensure you receive the fairest price. We work with trusted local couriers to get your order to your doorstep safely and efficiently.

Delivery Times

Once your order leaves our Dublin hub, here's what you can generally expect for delivery:

SAME-DAY DELIVERY (IMPORTANT!)

  • Availability: Strictly for addresses within Dublin City Centre (D01, D02, D03, D04, D07, D08 Eircodes only)
  • Cut-off Time: Orders placed by 12:00 PM GMT Monday to Friday are eligible
  • Delivery Window: Between 2:00 PM and 6:00 PM on the same business day
  • Weekend/Holiday Orders: Will be delivered on the next business day
  • Incorrect Selection: If you select this option for addresses outside Dublin City Centre, your order will be automatically adjusted to 'Greater Dublin & East' rate with delayed delivery

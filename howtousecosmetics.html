Phase 1

Pre-Tattoo Care

1

Important Aftercare Tips

Follow these guidelines for the best tattoo healing results

Keep It Clean

Wash the tattoo gently and keep it clean to prevent infections.

Moisturize Regularly

Apply Tattoo Balm/Tattoo Cream Derma daily to keep the tattoo hydrated and aid in healing.

Wash Hands First

Always wash your hands before touching your tattoo to avoid introducing bacteria.

Follow Artist's Advice

Stick to the specific aftercare instructions given by your tattoo artist for the best results.

Avoid Sun Exposure

Protect your tattoo from direct sunlight to prevent fading and damage.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water to help your skin heal and keep your tattoo looking its best.

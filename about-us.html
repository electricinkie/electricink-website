Our Story

Electric Ink was founded in Uberaba, Minas Gerais, Brazil, where the global Electric Ink brand brings together management, administration, sales, and communication activities. From their headquarters, they lead the creation, research, and development of tattoo machines, inks, needles, and electronic equipment.

Electric Ink Ireland operates as an online retailer, bringing the best of Electric Ink's globally-proven products to tattoo artists throughout Ireland. With over two decades of the parent company's industry experience and innovation, we provide Irish artists with premium tattoo supplies, exceptional customer service, and fast delivery across the country.

Excellence in Manufacturing

At Electric Ink, we design and manufacture every component in-house, ensuring precision, durability, and top-notch quality in all our products—from tattoo machines to inks and cosmetics for tattooing and micro-pigmentation. Certified by health organizations globally, including ANVISA in Brazil, we ensure all our products meet the highest safety standards.

For over 23 years, artists have trusted us. Today, we export to more than 30 countries. From the humble beginnings of a tattoo artist, a turner, and a few assistants creating the perfect machine, to our current team of almost 450 professionals, innovation, safety, and perfection have always been at the heart of what we do.

At Electric Ink, we are more than just equipment—we empower artists. Our products are designed to work in harmony, making the tattooing and micro-pigmentation process not only safer but also easier and more efficient for the professional and their clients.

Our Purpose

We are driven by a passion for innovation, always pushing the boundaries to deliver the highest quality products. We're committed to continuously evolving and creating tattoo supplies that exceed expectations—ensuring practicality, safety, and top-tier performance at every step.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in tattooing, not just through our products, but by fostering a culture of continuous improvement and collaboration with talented artists around the world. We aim to inspire, empower, and support the tattoo community through innovation and excellence.
